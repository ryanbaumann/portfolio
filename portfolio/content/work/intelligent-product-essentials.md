## The goal

Google Cloud wanted a repeatable entry into manufacturing, a vertical rich in infrastructure spend but without a packaged product story. The bar was a real solution with a marquee launch customer, not a reference architecture PDF.

## What shipped

I led product and engineering from zero to launch in nine months: Intelligent Product Essentials, combining edge AI and cloud analytics so manufacturers could ship smart, updatable, data-generating products. GE Appliances launched with us, and we built a $10M+ launch pipeline with business development, sales, and partner teams.

## What I learned

The discipline is the same at every altitude: work backwards from the adoption goal, ship the smallest artifact that proves the motion, then scale it through partners.
